Welcome
Student Travel Visualizer

Four steps to see how your school moves.

1
Build the Blueprint
Draw classrooms, hallways, and staircases. Pair staircases to connect floors.
2
Assign Rooms
Click any classroom and give it a room number — these become schedule destinations.
3
Add Schedules
Create groups and pick the room they visit each period of the day — A and B days supported.
4
Visualize Movement
Open Visualize and see paths with a green-to-red congestion heat scale across your building.
Quick Reference
Help & Tips
The workflow
1
Blueprint
Draw classrooms, hallways, and staircases. Pair staircases to teleport between disconnected sections. Right-click a tile for quick actions.
2
Rooms
Select a classroom and assign a unique room number. Room numbers must be unique across the building.
3
Schedules
Add groups, set a grade, and choose a destination room for each period. Set the number of periods in Settings.
4
Visualize
Choose Single, Comparison (up to 4), By Grade, or All. Busier hallways shift from green toward red.
